=== GigaCorp Shipping Policies ===
Line 1: GigaCorp offers standard, expedited, and international shipping options.
Line 2: Standard domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days and is free for orders over $50.
Line 3: Expedited domestic shipping takes 1-2 business days at a flat rate of $15.
Line 4: International shipping is available to select countries, including India, Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, Germany, and Singapore.
Line 5: Shipping to India costs a flat fee of $35 USD and usually takes 7-10 business days.
Line 6: Shipping to Canada costs $25 USD and takes approximately 5-8 business days.
Line 7: Orders placed before 5:00 PM EST are processed on the same business day.
Line 8: Orders placed after 5:00 PM EST are processed on the next business day.
Line 9: Customers receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once the order has shipped.
Line 10: Shipping delays may occur during holidays, severe weather, or customs inspections.
Line 11: Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.
Line 12: Shipping fees are refundable only if GigaCorp ships the wrong product or a damaged item.

=== GigaCorp Return Processes ===
Line 13: Customers can return most products within 30 days of purchase for a refund or exchange.
Line 14: To initiate a return, sign in to your GigaCorp account, open My Orders, and select Request Return.
Line 15: A prepaid return shipping label will be generated for eligible domestic returns.
Line 16: Products must be unused and returned in their original packaging.
Line 17: Electronics must include all original accessories and manuals.
Line 18: Personalized or customized products cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.
Line 19: Gift cards and digital downloads are non-refundable.
Line 20: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is inspected.
Line 21: Refunds are credited to the original payment method used during purchase.
Line 22: Exchanges depend on product availability at the time the return is processed.

=== GigaCorp Business Hours ===
Line 23: GigaCorp customer support is available Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Eastern Standard Time (EST).
Line 24: Live chat support is available during normal business hours.
Line 25: Email requests submitted outside business hours are answered on the next business day.
Line 26: Technical support is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for Enterprise customers.
Line 27: Our offices are closed on major United States public holidays including New Year's Day, Independence Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day.
Line 28: Average email response time for Basic customers is 24-48 hours.
Line 29: Average response time for Pro customers is under 4 hours.

Line 30: GigaCorp provides three subscription plans: Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.
Line 31: Basic is free and includes access to standard platform features and email support.
Line 32: Pro costs $29 per month and includes advanced features, priority email support, and live chat support.
Line 33: Enterprise pricing is customized according to business requirements.
Line 34: Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager.
Line 35: Enterprise customers receive 24/7 phone support.
Line 36: Enterprise customers can request custom software integrations.
Line 37: Pro customers receive early access to selected beta features.
Line 38: Basic customers do not receive phone support.

=== GigaCorp Billing & Payments ===

Line 40: Subscription plans renew automatically every month unless cancelled.
Line 41: Annual subscriptions receive a 10 percent discount compared to monthly billing.
Line 42: Customers may cancel subscriptions at any time from the Billing section of their account.
